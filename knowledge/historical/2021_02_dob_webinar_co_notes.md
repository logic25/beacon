# DOB Webinar Notes: DOB NOW Build - Certificate of Occupancies

**Date:** February 19, 2021
**Source:** Don Speaker's notes from DOB webinar, shared with GLE team
**Status:** Historical — some details may have been updated since 2021

## Key Takeaways from Webinar

### Schedule of Occupancy Requirement
- New Alt-1 filings MAY require the building to set up a **schedule of occupancy** on DOB NOW Build before filing
- Two methods to establish:
  1. **Official documentation** — submitted and verified by DOB
  2. **Current use arguments** — for pre-1938 buildings, logged as "unverified"
- TBD at time of webinar who is able to set up the initial building schedule of occupancy

### BIS to DOB NOW Build Transfer
- Open BIS C of O applications will be transferred to DOB NOW Build
- **Only the BIS applicant of record** can initiate the transfer
- Applicant of record must enter GLE's info as a contributor
- Once added and saved, GLE can access and complete the transfer
- Transfer timeline was anticipated as early as February 2021

### New C of O Types
- DOB introduced varying new C of O types including a **new interim C of O**
- Reference: Local Law 6 of 2021 (https://www.nyc.gov/assets/buildings/local_laws/ll6of2021.pdf)

### Additional Notes
- Most of the webinar covered technical navigation steps in DOB NOW Build
- Don noted some items with "?" marks indicating they needed confirmation
- Team was encouraged to sign up for future webinars to get full details
