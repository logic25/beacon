# 1 RCNY §101-14: Categories of Work That May or May Not Require a Permit

This rule establishes categories of work that may be classified as a minor alteration or ordinary repair and therefore may be exempt from permit requirements under the NYC Construction Codes.

**References:** AC §28-105.1, §28-105.4 item 11, §28-105.4.2.1

**Important:** Exemptions do not authorize work that violates the Building Code, Energy Conservation Code, Zoning Resolution, or other laws. Owners may still need to file at LPC, CPC, DOT, DEP, DEC, FDNY, or DOB for related work (sidewalk protection, structural, electrical, plumbing).

---

## Table 1: Work Exempt from Permit in 1- and 2-Family Dwellings

| Work | Permit Required? |
|---|---|
| **Sheds** — Portable freestanding, ≤120 SF, ≤7'6" height, ≥3' from lot line, storage use, max 1 per tax lot | NO |
| **Fences** — 6 feet or less in height | NO |
| **Small swimming pools** — Outdoor, ≤400 SF, pool edge-to-building/lot line distance > pool depth, with slop sink or plumbing permit | NO |
| **Playground equipment** — Accessory to 1-2 family | NO |
| **Packaged AC** — ≤5 tons, existing building, existing windows/sleeves, not part of other alteration | NO |

---

## Table 2: Work That May Be Exempt in All Buildings

### I. Exterior Structures

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| **Retaining walls** — <4' height, no surcharge, no Class I/II/III-A liquids | NO |
| **Nursery/agricultural shade cloth structures** — Not covering mechanical, plumbing, or autos | NO |

### II. Service Equipment

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| **AC/ventilating** — Voluntary system, 1 floor only, no lot line openings, not in hallway/stairway, no fire division/roof/floor/wall penetration | NO |
| **Packaged AC** — ≤3 tons, existing building, existing windows/sleeves | NO |
| **Portable fuel-burning** — No chimney/vent connection needed | NO |
| **Portable heaters during construction** — ≤6 gallon fuel storage | NO |
| **Oil-fired heaters** — ≤6 gallon fuel storage, not internal combustion | NO |
| **Refrigerating systems** — ≤25 tons, Group A1 refrigerant | NO |
| **Refrigerating systems** — In vehicle, railroad car, or vessel | NO |
| **Refrigerating systems** — Using water or air as refrigerant | NO |
| **Temporary boilers** — On private property or street (electrical/plumbing permits still required separately) | YES |

### III. Interior Partitions

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| **Non-fire-rated, non-load-bearing wall — TEMPORARY removal** — ≤50% of wall or ≤45 SF, restored to original | NO |
| **Non-fire-rated, non-load-bearing wall — PERMANENT removal in Group R** — ≤50% of wall surface or ≤45 SF | NO |
| **Non-fire-rated, non-load-bearing wall — PERMANENT removal in Groups B, M, S-1** — ≤50 linear feet in Type I or II construction | NO |

**EXCEPTION for Group R permanent removal — Permit IS required if:**
- Unit is in: Special Hudson Yards District, Preservation Area P-2 of Special Garment Center District, Special Clinton District, Special West Chelsea District, or Greenpoint-Williamsburg anti-harassment areas (CD1 Brooklyn)
- Unit is a single room occupancy multiple dwelling (requires building permit and Certificate of No Harassment per AC §28-107.4)

### IV. Roofs

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| **Roof repair above deck/sheathing** — Membrane, coverings, cant strip, insulation above deck (if Energy Code doesn't require additional insulation) | NO |
| **Roof repair including deck/sheathing** — Replacement of roof deck | YES |
| **Green roofs ≤4" depth** — On noncombustible construction or buildings >100' height | NO |

## Table 3: Facade Work That May Be Exempt in All Buildings

### I. Masonry (Not Including Terra Cotta and Stone)

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Brick repointing (or other unit masonry) | NO |
| Individual brick replacement — single outside wythe ≤10 SF, ≤4' horizontal, per 100 SF wall area, cumulative ≤150 SF all facades | NO |
| Mechanical anchorage (pinning) of brick to underlying structure | YES |
| Parapet demolition and reconstruction | YES |
| Increasing height of existing parapet | YES |
| New parapet coping (masonry) | NO |
| New parapet guardrail | YES |
| Replacement of existing guardrail/parapet to same height (masonry parapets: ≤10 SF per 100 SF continuous parapet) | NO |
| Expansion/control joints in existing masonry (saw-cutting) | YES |
| Flashing, weeps, relieving angles/lintels, new brickwork exceeding limits in #2 | YES |
| Flashing: reglet cutting, removing 1-2 courses inside parapet face, exceeding limits | YES |
| Masonry crack repair with injection mortar | NO |
| Masonry cladding of existing exterior walls | YES |
| Replacement of masonry sills | NO |

### II. Concrete Restoration

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Structural repair/reinforcement (re-bars, post-tension cables, curtain panels, precast) | YES |
| Spandrel repair — cracked/spalled concrete on spandrels, fascias, balconies | YES |
| Re-anchoring existing aluminum/steel balcony handrails | NO |
| Concrete crack repair with injection cement | NO |

### III. Stone/Terra Cotta Restoration

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Patching spalls/cracks on exterior stone or terra cotta (buildings subject to §28-302) | YES |
| Removal/replacement of exterior stone or terra cotta ornamentation with alternative material | YES |
| Mechanical anchorage (pinning) of displaced stone or terra cotta | YES |

### IV. Curtain/Panel Walls

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Repair/replacement of structural components of panel wall system | YES |
| Removal/replacement of inspection plates | NO |
| Repair/replacement of sheet metal window clip covers | NO |
| Replacement of exterior glass panels in kind — ≤1,000 SF per 10,000 SF wall area | NO |
| Replacement of inner glass panels from within building | NO |

### V. Cleaning/Coating of Masonry/Concrete/Metal/EIFS

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Cleaning, coloring, or painting masonry/concrete | NO |
| Cementitious material (stucco) ≤1" or brownstone ≤1/8", limited to 40' height from grade or 10'+ deep setback, excluding new EIFS | NO |
| Sealant, caulking, regrouting | NO |
| Minor EIFS repair — ≤10 SF per 100 SF continuous EIFS wall | NO |

### VI. Miscellaneous Metal/Steel

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Scraping/painting, flashing/coloring, sealing/coating steel members (including lintels) with masonry removal >10 SF, >4' horizontal, per 100 SF (buildings subject to §28-302) | YES |
| Removal/replacement of steel structural members >10 SF, >4' horizontal, per 100 SF exterior wall | YES |
| Reinforcement of steel structural members (spandrels, beams, columns) | YES |
| Reinforcement of fire escape bars, struts, baskets, supports | NO |
| Installation of handrails at balconies, terraces, rooftops | YES |
| Signs >6 SF, flagpoles, water tanks, awnings, satellite dishes on exterior/balconies/terraces/rooftops (Exception: flagpoles, awnings, satellite dishes for 1-2 family = NO permit) | YES |

### VII. Sheet Metal

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Sheet metal cladding of existing cornice facades | YES |
| Localized non-structural repairs/patching to sheet metal cornice | NO |
| Repair/reinforcement of structural supports of sheet metal cornices | YES |
| New replacement sheet metal or GFRC cornice | YES |
| Parapet coping cover (sheet metal) | NO |
| Sheet metal cladding of exposed parapet wall surfaces | YES |

### VIII. Doors/Windows

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Window replacement — not on lot line, no masonry opening modification, no light/ventilation impact, no change in operable area | NO |
| Replacement of required fire-rated windows | YES |
| Repair/replacement of window and door sills | NO |

### IX. Miscellaneous

| Work | Permit? |
|---|---|
| Probes — <10 SF masonry removal (≤4' horizontal) per 100 SF, cumulative ≤150 SF all facades | NO |
| Replacement in kind of exterior gutters and leaders | NO |
| Removal of existing signs and marquees | NO |
| Replacement of existing signs and marquees | YES |
| Installation of new signs and marquees (other than painted) | YES |
| Open screen balcony enclosures — <40' above grade | NO |
| Weather-resistant balcony enclosures (windows, solid walls) | YES |
