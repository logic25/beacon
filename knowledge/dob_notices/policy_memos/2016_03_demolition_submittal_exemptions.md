# Demolition Submittal Document Requirements: Exemption from Plan Submission

**March 2016** — DOB will no longer accept plan submissions for sites exempt from plan submission under the Code. These are exemptions from **plan submissions only** — a demolition application (DM) is still required.

## Full Demolitions: 1-, 2-, and 3-Family Homes

Demolition submittal documents NOT required if demolition is limited to **handheld devices** (including handheld mechanical devices) of the above-grade portions of:

1. A **detached** 1-, 2-, or 3-family dwelling, 3 stories or less
2. **Both halves** of a **semi-detached** 1-, 2-, or 3-family dwelling, 3 stories or less
3. A **detached accessory structure** (e.g., detached garage) to a 1-, 2-, or 3-family dwelling — regardless of whether the main dwelling is detached or also being demolished

## Foundation Removals: 1-, 2-, and 3-Family Homes

After demolition to grade, foundation can be removed **without** demolition submittal documents if:
- Using handheld devices OR mechanical equipment
- Mechanical equipment is NOT supported on any existing structure other than a slab on grade (slab supported by soil)

**Note:** Protection requirements still apply — sheeting/shoring, underpinning adjoining property, backfilling, etc.

## Full Demolitions: Detached Buildings (Other Than 1-2-3 Family)

No demolition submittal documents required if ALL three conditions met:
1. Demolition limited to **handheld devices** (including handheld mechanical)
2. Building is **3 stories or less** in height
3. Floor area is **less than 5,000 SF per floor**

**Critical:** Both above-grade AND foundation must be removed with handheld devices. **Using an excavator or other non-handheld device to remove the foundation TRIGGERS the need for demolition submittal documents.**

## Partial Demolitions

Partial demolition operations limited to handheld devices performed during a **minor alteration or ordinary repair** do NOT require demolition submittal documents.

## Key Takeaways

- A DM application is ALWAYS still required — this only exempts the plan submission
- "Handheld devices" is the key trigger — the moment you bring in an excavator for anything other than 1-2-3 family foundation removal, you need full submittal documents
- For non-residential detached buildings, the 5,000 SF/floor AND 3-story limits both apply
- Semi-detached buildings: BOTH halves must be demolished, not just one side
