# DOB Fee Updates in DOB NOW: Build (Effective February 2, 2026)

**Issued:** January 8, 2026  
**Effective Date:** February 2, 2026  
**Department:** NYC Department of Buildings  
**Notice Type:** Service Notice

---

## Summary

Beginning February 2, 2026, DOB NOW: Build will update fee calculations for:
1. Subsequent filings on Alt-CO jobs (New Buildings with Existing Elements to Remain)
2. New Full Demolition job filings
3. AHV permits

---

## 1. Subsequent Filings on Alt-CO Jobs

**What Changed:**
- **OLD:** Flat fee of $130 for all subsequent filings
- **NEW:** Fees calculated based on construction cost reported on filing

**Applies to these work types:**
- Plumbing
- Sprinkler
- Standpipe
- General Construction
- Mechanical Systems
- Structural
- Support of Excavation
- Protection and Mechanical

### Fee Calculation Examples

**For 1, 2, or 3-Family Buildings:**
- Construction cost: $20,000
- Calculation: $170 for first $5,000 + ($2.60 per $1,000 over $5,000)
- **New Base Filing Fee: $209.00**

**For Buildings Below 7 Floors:**
- Construction cost: $20,000
- Calculation: $280 for first $3,000 + ($10.30 per $1,000 over $3,000)
- **New Base Filing Fee: $455.10**

**For Buildings 7 Floors & Above:**
- Construction cost: $20,000
- Calculation: $290 for first $3,000 + ($17.75 per $1,000 over $3,000)
- **New Base Filing Fee: $591.75**

**When This Applies:**
- Only for NEW subsequent filings submitted on or after February 2, 2026
- Does NOT apply to filings already in system before Feb 2

---

## 2. Full Demolition Filings

**What Changed:**
- **OLD:** No Record Management Fee
- **NEW:** Record Management Fee added based on building type

**New Fees:**
- **1, 2, or 3-Family buildings:** $45
- **All other buildings:** $165

**When This Applies:**
- New Full Demolition initial filings submitted on or after February 2, 2026

---

## 3. AHV Permit Fees

**What Changed:**
- **OLD:** Fee-exempt jobs had no AHV fees (except government-owned)
- **NEW:** Fee-exempt jobs will be charged standard AHV fee

**Exception:**
- Government-owned properties remain fee-exempt

**When This Applies:**
- New AHV filings submitted on or after February 2, 2026

---

## Impact on GLE Projects

### Alt-CO Subsequent Filings

**Before Feb 2, 2026:**
- Client budget: $130 per subsequent filing
- Predictable flat fee

**After Feb 2, 2026:**
- Client budget: Varies by construction cost
- Example: $20K project = $209-$592 depending on building type
- **Action:** Update client quotes to reflect variable DOB fees

### Full Demolition Projects

**New cost to include:**
- Add $45 (1-3 family) or $165 (other) to DOB fee estimates
- **Action:** Update demolition pricing to include Record Management Fee

### AHV Permits

**For fee-exempt projects (non-government):**
- Now include standard AHV permit fee in quotes
- **Action:** Verify if project qualifies for government exemption

## How to Handle Client Questions

**Q: "Why did my subsequent filing fee go up?"**
**A:** "DOB implemented new fee calculations on Feb 2, 2026. Instead of a flat $130, fees now scale based on your project's construction cost. For your $20K project, the DOB fee is now $[calculated amount]."

**Q: "Can we file before Feb 2 to get the old rate?"**
**A:** "If your filing is submitted and entered into DOB NOW before Feb 2, 2026, the old $130 flat fee applies. After Feb 2, the new calculation applies."

**Q: "How do I know what my fee will be?"**
**A:** "We can calculate it based on your construction cost and building type. Share your project details and we'll provide an exact DOB fee estimate."
