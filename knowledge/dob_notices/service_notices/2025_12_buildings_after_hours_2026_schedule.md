# Buildings After Hours 2026 Schedule

**Issued:** December 17, 2025  
**Commissioner:** James S. Oddo

## Schedule Overview

Buildings After Hours sessions are scheduled for the **first and third Tuesday of each month** from **4:00 PM to 7:00 PM** throughout 2026.

**Important:** If the scheduled Tuesday falls on a government holiday, Buildings After Hours will be **cancelled** for that date.

## What is Buildings After Hours?

Buildings After Hours is a public service program designed for:
- Homeowners
- Tenants
- Building managers
- Small business owners

## Services Available

Attendees can visit their **local DOB borough office** to:

1. **Ask questions** about construction or renovation projects
2. **Plan home renovations** or construction projects with staff guidance
3. **Address open violations** on their property
4. **Obtain property information** from DOB records
5. **Learn to use online resources:**
   - Buildings Information System (BIS)
   - DOB NOW portal

## Where to Go

Visit your local DOB borough office during scheduled hours. Staff will be available to provide one-on-one assistance.

## Practical Notes for GLE Team

**Client referral opportunity:** When homeowners or small property owners contact GLE about simple questions or research tasks, consider referring them to Buildings After Hours first if their needs are basic (violation lookups, general filing questions, DOB NOW navigation). This positions GLE for higher-value work while building goodwill.

**November exception:** Note the Election Day closure for the first Tuesday in November. If scheduling client meetings or expecting DOB staff availability, avoid November 3rd.

**Hours are evening-friendly:** 4-7 PM allows working clients to attend. If a client mentions they "can't get to DOB during business hours," this is the answer.
