# New Restrictions on Structural Permits in DOB NOW

**Effective February 2, 2026** — Per Buildings Bulletin 2025-011, DOB NOW will restrict the issuance and renewal of Structural permits connected to a new building, enlargement, or change in use but filed as a separate Alteration filing.

## Filing Requirements

For separately filed Alteration filings in DOB NOW, applicants must answer a new PW1 question:

**"Is this Alteration being filed in connection with a development, enlargement, or change in use on this BIN, as defined in Zoning Resolution Section 12-10?"**

- If **No**: Filing proceeds as usual.
- If **Yes**: Applicant must provide one of the following job numbers:
  - New Building (NB)
  - Alt-CO - New Building with existing building elements to remain (NBEE)
  - Alteration-CO (Alt-CO) with ZD1
  - Alteration with an enlargement

Additionally, if the Alteration filing includes Work Type **Structural (ST)**, **Foundation (FO)**, or **Earthwork (EA)**, the applicant must provide the **Determination Number** for an approved Determination to proceed.

## Permit Issuance Rules

| Work Type | Condition for Permit Issuance |
|-----------|-------------------------------|
| FO or EA | ZD1 on related NB, NBEE, Alt-CO, or Alt w/Enlargement must be in **Accepted** status |
| ST (related job is NB, NBEE, or Alt-CO with ZD1) | GC permit on the **Initial filing (I1)** for the related job must be issued |
| ST (related job is Alt w/Enlargement) | GC permit on the **related job** must be issued |

## Permit Renewal

If the PW1 answer is Yes and the Alteration filing includes Work Type ST, the **GC permit of the related job must be active** (not expired) to renew the ST permit.

## Practical Impact

- Separate structural filings connected to NB/enlargement/change-of-use projects now require linking to the main job
- Foundation and earthwork permits are blocked until zoning is accepted
- Structural permits are blocked until the GC permit on the main job is issued
- Expired GC permits on the main job will prevent ST permit renewals on the separate Alt filing
- Plan ahead: ensure the main job ZD1 and GC permit are in order before filing separate structural work
