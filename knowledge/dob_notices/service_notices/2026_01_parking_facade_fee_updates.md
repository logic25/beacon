# Fee Updates for Parking Structure Compliance Reports and Facade Inspection Reports in DOB NOW: Safety

**Effective February 2, 2026** — DOB NOW: Safety will charge new and increased fees per amendments to 1 RCNY Sections 101-03, 103-04, and 103-13.

## New Fees (Previously Free)

| Request Type | Compliance Filing Type | Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Partial Protection Removal Request | Parking Structures | $695 |
| Verification Request | Parking Structures | $685 |
| Civil Penalty Waiver Request | Parking Structures | $140 |
| Civil Penalty Waiver Request | Facades | $140 |

## Increased Fees - Parking Structure Compliance Reports

| Report/Request Type | Old Fee | New Fee | Increase |
|---|---|---|---|
| Initial Filing | $305 | $485 | +$180 |
| Subsequent Filing | $85 | $485 | +$400 |
| Amended Filing | $85 | $940 | +$855 |
| Repair Extension Request | $65 | $95 | +$30 |

## Key Details

- Updated fees apply to all **new filings** created in DOB NOW: Safety on or after February 2, 2026
- Filings in **Pre filing status with a fee due** will also get the new fees
- Filings in Pre filing status that have **already been paid** keep the old fee
- Subsequent filing fees jumped dramatically from $85 to $485 — factor this into client estimates for multi-filing projects

## Pricing Impact for GLE

- Parking structure compliance work now costs significantly more in DOB fees
- Amended filings nearly 11x the old cost ($85 → $940) — warn clients about amendment costs upfront
- Facade civil penalty waiver requests now have a $140 fee where there was none before
- Update fee schedules in all parking structure and facade proposals
