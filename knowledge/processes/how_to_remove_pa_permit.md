# How to Remove a Place of Assembly (PA) Permit

## Overview

When a Place of Assembly (PA) space is being completely eliminated, an FDNY inspection is required to remove the PA account from FDNY's system.

**When you need this:**
- Converting PA space to different use
- Eliminating assembly use entirely
- Closing a venue/restaurant permanently
- Removing PA designation after building renovation

---

## Prerequisites

Before starting, you need:
- PA account number (from original permit)
- Building address and block/lot
- FDNY Business account (free to create)

**Don't have PA account number?**
- Search using FDNY Building Profile Guide
- Or call FDNY Business Support: 311
- Or email: FDNY.BusinessSupport@fdny.nyc.gov

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Create FDNY Business Account (if needed)

**URL:** https://fires.fdnycloud.org/CitizenAccess/Default.aspx

1. Click **"Create an Account"**
2. Enter business information:
   - Company name
   - Contact person
   - Email (will be your login)
   - Phone number
3. Verify email address
4. Complete account setup
5. Log in with credentials

**Time:** 10-15 minutes

---

### Step 2: Find Your PA Account Number

**If you don't have the account number:**

1. Log into FDNY Business
2. Use **"Building Profile Search"** feature
3. Enter building address
4. Review list of FDNY accounts for that building
5. Identify PA account (will show "Public Assembly" type)
6. Note the account number (format: PA-XXXXXX)

**Resources:**
- FDNY Building Profile Guide (PDF available from FDNY)
- FDNY Business Support: 311

---

### Step 3: Request Inspection

1. **Log into FDNY Business**
   https://fires.fdnycloud.org/CitizenAccess/Default.aspx

2. **Select "Begin Application / Request"**

3. **Choose inspection unit:**
   - Select: **"Public Assembly"**

4. **Select inspection type:**
   - Choose: **"PA Removal"** or **"Decommission"**

5. **CRITICAL:** Enter PA account number
   - Section: "Inspection Information"
   - Field: "Inspection Record ID/Account#"
   - Enter: Your PA account number (PA-XXXXXX)
   - **DO NOT SKIP THIS** - without account number, request will be rejected

6. **Provide additional information:**
   - Reason for removal
   - Current building use
   - Contact information for site access

7. **Submit request**

8. **Confirmation**
   - You'll receive confirmation email
   - Reference number for tracking

---

### Step 4: Schedule & Complete Inspection

**After submission:**

1. **FDNY will contact you** (usually within 5-7 business days)
   - Via email or phone
   - To schedule inspection date/time

2. **Prepare for inspection:**
   - Ensure site access available
   - Have contact person on-site
   - Space should already be converted (PA use eliminated)

3. **Inspection day:**
   - Inspector verifies PA space no longer exists/in use
   - Inspector documents space condition
   - May take photos
   - Typical inspection: 30-60 minutes

4. **Inspection result:**
   - Inspector approves removal OR
   - Inspector identifies issues to resolve

---

### Step 5: Account Closure

**After successful inspection:**

1. FDNY processes removal (3-5 business days)
2. PA account closed in FDNY system
3. Confirmation issued via email
4. Annual PA Certificate of Operation (C of O) requirement removed
5. No further PA renewals/inspections required

---

## Timeline

**Total process:** 2-4 weeks

- **FDNY Business account setup:** 15 minutes
- **Inspection request submission:** 30 minutes
- **FDNY review & scheduling:** 5-7 business days
- **Inspection completion:** 1 day
- **Account closure processing:** 3-5 business days

**Expedited:** Not typically available for PA removals

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Can't find PA account number
**Problem:** Don't have original permit or account info  
**Solution:** 
- Use FDNY Building Profile search
- Call FDNY Business Support: 311
- Provide building address - they can locate account
- May need to visit FDNY in person (9 MetroTech Center, Brooklyn)

### Issue 2: Multiple PA accounts at building
**Problem:** Building has several PA spaces, need to remove just one  
**Solution:**
- Identify specific PA account for your space (by floor/suite)
- Submit separate removal request for each account
- Clearly indicate which space in request notes

### Issue 3: Inspection fails - PA use still exists
**Problem:** Inspector finds PA use still active or space not converted  
**Solution:**
- Complete conversion work first
- Submit DOB filing if needed for space change
- Re-request inspection after work complete

### Issue 4: FDNY says DOB approval required first
**Problem:** Space change requires DOB approval before PA removal  
**Solution:**
- File Alt-1 or Alt-2 with DOB for change of use
- Get DOB approval/sign-off
- Then request FDNY PA removal
- GLE can coordinate both processes

---

## Important Notes

**⚠️ You CANNOT:**
- Remove PA permit without FDNY inspection
- Simply stop filing annual PA certificates (account stays active)
- Have DOB remove PA - must go through FDNY

**✅ You MUST:**
- Request inspection through FDNY Business portal ONLY
- Enter PA account number in request
- Have actual elimination of PA use complete before inspection

**💡 Pro Tip:**
If converting PA to another use requiring DOB approval, coordinate:
1. DOB filing & approval first
2. Complete construction/conversion
3. Then request FDNY PA removal
4. Then request DOB final sign-off/CO amendment
